THE SHUTTER CIRCLE

The Shutter Circle is a community and platform for people who love film photography. In a world where digital photography is everywhere, we focus on the unique qualities of shooting with film and the skills it takes to work with analog cameras.

Our goal is to bring together photographers of all levels—from beginners to experienced shooters—to share knowledge, tips, and stories about film photography. Whether you’re learning how to use your first 35mm camera or have been developing prints in a darkroom for years, you’ll find helpful resources and a supportive community here.

We offer tutorials, gear advice, and features on different types of film and developing processes. We also highlight photographers’ projects and personal stories to show how film photography is still relevant and inspiring today. The Shutter Circle organizes events like photowalks and workshops to help members connect and learn from each other in person. We believe that being part of a community is important for growth and creativity.

No matter what format or style you prefer, The Shutter Circle is a place to learn, share, and enjoy film photography. Join us and be part of a group that values every shot and every story behind it.
